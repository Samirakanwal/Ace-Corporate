About us

Ace Corporate, is one of the leading consultancy and advisory firm based out of the twin cities of Rawalpindi and Islamabad. The firm is enriched with the professional competence in the area of Tax and Business advisory and provides tax, enterprise risk services, financial advisory and development sector consulting to a wide range of local and multinational clients. Established in 1980, the firm is one of the pioneer organization in the twin cites for providing professional business consultancy services.

The firm has provided services to over 500 clients including both multinational and local enterprises & individuals. The firm has significant presence across International Development Coorperation Agencies working in Pakistan as well as their local partner organizations in the country. Our team includes qualified accountants, Lawyers, MBAs, IT experts and other technically qualified persons.

Ace Corporate brings together clients, offers innovative solutions, and endeavors to exceed client expectations. The firm adopts a customized, responsive and personal approach towards client service and offers a complete range of services across various industries.

Ace Corporate is a trusted name for providing valuable Financial and Management advisory services. We help our clients handle all financial, Internal Control and Management matters swiftly at all levels. We provide clients with a full range of resources required to handle financial internal controls design, financial capacity building, financial & system audits and direct and indirect taxes and tax management problems.

The professional team at Ace Corporate counsel on the application of laws, rules and regulations to define the effect of government policies on long range plans and assists clients with rulings, filings, registrations, applications and other dealings with government offices.

Ace Corporate also specializes in providing capacity buliding services to the public sector organizations engaged in the revenue collection and it allied services. It has served different provincial tax collection departments with the support of International Development Agencies and successfully completed projects for the uplift of functional and operational capacites of public revenue authorities.

We enjoy excellent professional relations with all the concerned government and autonomous Authorities including:

  • Federal Board of Revenue (FBR)
  • Punjab Revenue Authority (PRA)
  • Khyber Pakhtunkhwa Revenue Authority (KPRA)
  • Securities and Exchange Commission of Pakistan (SECP)
  • Pakistna Stock Exchange (PSX)
  • Trade Development Authority of Pakistan (TDAP)
  • Chambers of Commerce and Industries
